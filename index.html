About Dash Chat

We’re developing an offline-first, encrypted, peer-to-peer messaging app specifically designed for communities experiencing internet shutdowns, low connectivity, natural disasters, or unreliable network conditions.

With authoritarianism and network interference increasing worldwide, secure and sovereign communication without dependence on stable internet is becoming essential yet remains a challenge.

Dash Chat operates independently from centralized servers or stable internet, built directly with communities most affected by network disruptions, ensuring encrypted communication precisely when it matters most.

Why Another Messaging App?

  • Signal, Telegram, Matrix: Fail during internet shutdowns.
  • Delta Chat: Resilient, but dependent on email infrastructure.
  • Briar: No iOS, multi-device, backups, or multimedia.

Dash Chat aims to recreate main-net chat experiences reliably on local networks, prioritizing usability and security.

Who It’s For

  • Communities facing poor or disrupted internet infrastructure
  • Villages in the global south, disaster-affected communities
  • Protestors, journalists, human rights defenders, digital security practitioners
